## 2b, ALP to accept number from user through keyboard , calculate its factorial and print on screen.

```js
title "factorial of a number"

.model small
.stack 100h

.code
    mov ah, 1
    int 21h
    mov bl, al

again: dec bl
    mul bl
    cmp bl, 1
    jnz again
    mov dl, al
    mov ah, 2
    int 21h

    mov ax, 4c00h
    int21h
end
```
